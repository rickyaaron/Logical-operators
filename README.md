********Logical operators**********

print("Logical Operations:")

print(f"{a} > {b} and {a} != 0: {a > b and a != 0}")

print(f"{a} > {b} or {a} == 0: {a > b or a == 0}")

print(f"not({a} > {b}): {not (a > b)}")

print("-----------------------------------")

O/P:

Logical Operations:

10 > 5 and 10 != 0: True

10 > 5 or 10 == 0: True

not(10 > 5): False# Logical-operators
